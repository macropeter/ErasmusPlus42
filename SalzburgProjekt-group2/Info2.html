Difference between BORG Nonntal and IIS Olivetti

GRADES

Italian grades are distributed on a decimal scale (1-10) and 10 is the maximum number that can be achieved,

Austrian grades range from 1 to 5 with maximum grade 1.

Difference in length of lessons

In the Ivrean School lessons last 48 minutes

Salzburg the lessons last 50 minutes.

Breaks

in the Austrian School there are 5 intervals whose durations are: 5 minutes, 10 minutes, 15 minutes, 5 minutes, 5 minutes. while those in the Austrian school last 50 minutes.

The intervals in the Ivrean School are two, which last ten minutes each and occur every two modules. Lunch breaks in the Italian school last 48 minutes.

High school route duration

the Austrian school route which has a duration of 4 years.

School day duration

The Ivrean school provides only one afternoon during the week until 3:30 pm, three days classes end at 1:10 pm, and the fifth day lasts until 12:20 pm

the school in Salzburg provides three afternoons per week that last until 4:15 pm, and the remaining two days end at 1:40 pm.

Footwear

the Ivrean school only shoes can be worn, which are also used outdoor.

The only footwear allowed in the school in Salzburg is shoes or slippers used in the school building
